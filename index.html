Our Story

TENNACITY
COMES
TO OC.

Tennacity built its reputation in West Hollywood on a simple idea: great coaching, open courts, and a community that pushes you further.

Now we're bringing that same DNA to Costa Mesa at 880 Junipero Dr — nestled alongside beautiful TeWinkle Park — 12 lit hard courts and a team that lives and breathes racquet sports.

This is home now. We can't wait to see you on the court.

Who We Are

ABOUT TENNACITY
COSTA MESA

Tennacity started in the heart of West Hollywood and became one of Southern California's premier racquet sports destinations — known for elite instruction, inclusive programming, and a community-first culture that welcomes players at every level.

Now we're planting our flag in Costa Mesa at 880 Junipero Dr — the Costa Mesa Tennis Center, a 10-court tennis and 8-court pickleball facility nestled alongside TeWinkle Park, one of the most beautiful outdoor sports settings in Orange County.

Our staff includes coaches who have worked with Grand Slam champions, former USTA Player Development coaches who shaped the next generation of American tennis, and pickleball professionals building a thriving competitive community right here in OC.

Whether you're picking up a racquet for the first time or you're a seasoned competitor, Tennacity Costa Mesa is your home court.

The People

MEET THE
TEAM

From Grand Slam coaches to OC locals, our team brings world-class expertise and genuine passion for the game to every session at 880 Junipero Dr.

🎾 Tennis Staff
Chris Tontz
Head Tennis Director

Former ATP touring professional and UC Irvine standout — #1 in both singles and doubles for the Anteaters. Reached a career-high ATP doubles ranking of 162, won a Challenger title in Grenoble, and defeated players including Lleyton Hewitt and Ivan Ljubičić on tour. As a USTA Player Development coach, Chris coached Sloane Stephens to a US Open title and world No. 3 ranking, and has also worked with Claire Liu. One of the most credentialed coaches in Southern California.

Javier Pulgar
Tennis Coach

Spanish professional coach known for developing elite junior talent into ATP-caliber professionals. Best known as the longtime coach of Brandon Nakashima — guiding him from junior development to a career-high ATP ranking of No. 29 and the 2022 Next Gen ATP Finals title. A tactician who focuses on technical precision and competitive mentality, Javier brings proven methodology to Tennacity's junior and advanced programs.

Adam Peterson
Tennis Coach & Advisory Board

A Costa Mesa native who grew up playing right here at this very facility — making this role a true homecoming. Former USTA Player Development coach who has coached Lindsay Davenport, Madison Keys, and Julieta Pareja (world #1 junior). Adam's deep roots in Orange County tennis and his elite pedigree make him a cornerstone of this program.

🏸 Pickleball Staff
Max Osswald
Pickleball Director

Max leads both the Pickleball program and overall facility operations at Tennacity Costa Mesa. A passionate competitor and organizer, Max built the pickleball community from the ground up — creating league structures, DUPR-rated events, and a welcoming culture for players of every skill level across Orange County.

Patrick Strom
Pickleball Coach

Patrick brings energy, precision, and a deep love of the game to every clinic and league session. Specializing in strategy, court positioning, and competitive play, he works with players from beginners learning the basics to seasoned competitors aiming to climb the DUPR rankings.

⚙️ Management
Josh Osswald
Operations Manager

Josh manages the behind-the-scenes operations that keep Tennacity Costa Mesa running smoothly — facility maintenance, vendor relationships, and member experience. Detail-oriented and solutions-focused, he's the engine that keeps everything moving.

Anniken Aalbu

Anniken oversees all daily operations at Tennacity Costa Mesa — from court scheduling and staff management to community events and partnerships. Her vision is to make Costa Mesa the best racquet sports destination in Orange County for players of every background and ability level.

⭐ Advisory Board

Costa Mesa native, former USTA Player Development coach. Coached Lindsay Davenport, Madison Keys, and world #1 junior Julieta Pareja. Brings unmatched local roots and elite coaching pedigree to guide Tennacity's tennis direction.

Chris Tontz

Steve Nash

Two-time NBA MVP, Basketball Hall of Famer (2018), and passionate tennis player who has competed in open division tournaments. Steve brings his love of sport, wellness philosophy, and community-building mission — plus the reach of the Steve Nash Foundation — to Tennacity.

Boris Kodjoe

Actor, model, and former collegiate tennis standout who played on scholarship at Virginia Commonwealth University — 9th in school history in singles wins. Boris brings his passion for tennis, his platform, and his commitment to excellence to the Tennacity family.
